Words of Radiance Overview


Main Characters

Kaladin's arc in the second book is marked by his struggle to come to terms with his identity as a Knight Radiant, a wielder of powerful Surgebinding abilities. Initially skeptical of his own worthiness and haunted by past failures, Kaladin undergoes a journey of self-discovery and acceptance as he embraces his role as a protector and leader. Kaladin's relationships with other characters, particularly Shallan and Dalinar, also play a significant role in his arc. His interactions with Shallan challenge his perceptions and beliefs, while his growing admiration for Dalinar inspires him to rise above his own limitations and strive for greatness.
Kaladin

Shallan's story in the second book delves into her struggle to come to terms with her traumatic past and the secrets that haunt her family. As she delves deeper into her studies under Jasnah Kholin, Shallan's abilities as a Lightweaver, a type of Radiant, begin to manifest, allowing her to create illusions and manipulate perception. As Shallan uncovers hidden truths about her family and the world around her, she becomes increasingly entangled in the political intrigue and ancient mysteries that shape the fate of Roshar. Her quest for knowledge and understanding drives her to confront her own fears and insecurities, ultimately leading her on a path of self-discovery and empowerment.
Shallan Davar

Despite facing skepticism and opposition from those around him, Dalinar remains steadfast in his commitment to uniting the Alethi highprinces and preparing for the coming Desolation. As Dalinar delves deeper into the mysteries of the Voidbringers and the ancient history of Roshar, he uncovers startling revelations that challenge his understanding of the world and his place in it. His relationships with other characters, including Kaladin and Shallan, provide opportunities for introspection and growth as he navigates the complexities of leadership and duty.
Dalinar Kholin

Szeth-son-son-Vallano continues to serve as an assassin, executing orders given to him by whoever holds his Oathstone. Throughout the narrative, Szeth carries out several high-profile assassinations, including attacks on prominent figures within the Alethi nobility. However, as the story progresses, Szeth's arc becomes more complex. He encounters characters like Dalinar Kholin and Kaladin Stormblessed, who challenge his beliefs and force him to confront the consequences of his actions. Szeth begins to question his role as a Truthless and the validity of the commands he blindly follows. Additionally, Szeth's own internal struggles and conflicts are brought to the forefront as he grapples with the weight of his choices and the possibility of redemption. His encounters with other characters, as well as the revelations he uncovers about the nature of his powers, contribute to his evolving character arc throughout the book.
Szeth-son-son-Velano



Way of Kings Plot Summary


Kaladin Stormblessed, now a respected member of the Alethi military, struggles with the weight of his newfound powers as a Knight Radiant. As he grapples with his responsibilities as a leader and protector, Kaladin faces internal turmoil stemming from his past traumas and the societal expectations placed upon him. Shallan Davar, meanwhile, continues her studies under the mentorship of Jasnah Kholin, delving into the mysteries of Surgebinding and uncovering dark secrets about her family's past. As she navigates the intricacies of her own abilities as a Lightweaver, Shallan finds herself drawn into a web of political intrigue and personal revelation. At the heart of the narrative is Dalinar Kholin, a highprince haunted by visions of a lost Radiant society and driven by a desire to unite the fractured Alethi kingdoms against the looming threat of the Voidbringers. As Dalinar delves deeper into the enigmatic messages of the past, he confronts his own inner demons and grapples with the complexities of leadership and honor.




Side-Characters


Jasnah Kholin - Daughter of Dalinar Kholin and Shallans mentor throughout the story.
Adolin Kholin - son of Dalinar Kholin and Brother of Jasnah.
Navani Kholin - Mother of the King of Alethkar, Elhokar, and Wife of the late king of Alethkar, Gavilar.
Wit - also known as Hoid, plays a multifaceted and enigmatic role that extends beyond mere entertainment and storytelling. As a recurring character throughout the narrative, Wit serves as a catalyst for introspection, revelation, and personal growth for many of the main characters.
