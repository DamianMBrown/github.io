About the Author

Brandon Sanderson is a highly acclaimed American author primarily known for his work in the fantasy genre. He's renowned for his prolific output, intricate world-building, and unique magic systems. His major works and contributions such as the Stormlight Archive, Mistborn Series, Elantris, Skyward Series, and more are seen as puzzle pieces to the overall Cosmere universe, where these stories interact with one another.


The Cosmere

The Cosmere is a vast and interconnected universe created by American author Brandon Sanderson. It serves as the overarching setting for many of his fantasy novels and series. At its core, the Cosmere is a collection of different planets, each with its own unique magic systems, cultures, and histories. What makes the Cosmere particularly intriguing is the presence of overarching cosmological elements and a grand narrative that spans across various series.

Shards

Shards are powerful entities that represent fundamental aspects of creation. When a Shards' vessel dies or chooses to release their power, the Shard can be splintered or held by another individual. These Shards play a significant role in shaping the worlds within the Cosmere.

Investiture and Magic Systems

Investiture is the energy or power source that fuels magic systems within the Cosmere. Each world has its own unique form of Investiture, resulting in distinct magic systems with specific rules and limitations. Examples include Allomancy and Feruchemy in the Mistborn series, Surgebinding in The Stormlight Archive, and BioChromatic Breath in Warbreaker.

World Hopping and Crossover Characters

Certain characters within the Cosmere possess the ability to travel between different planets or realms. This allows for crossover appearances and references between Sanderson's various series, enriching the overall narrative and world-building.

Cosmere-aware Characters

Some characters within individual series become aware of the larger Cosmere and its overarching events. These characters often play significant roles in connecting different storylines and understanding the deeper mysteries of the universe.

Shards' Intent and Conflict

The intentions of Shards and their interactions with mortal beings drive much of the conflict and plot progression within the Cosmere. These conflicts often explore themes of power, morality, and the consequences of wielding god-like abilities.


The Stormlight Archive

"The Stormlight Archive" by Brandon Sanderson is an epic fantasy series set in the world of Roshar. "The Way of Kings" introduces characters like Kaladin, Shallan, and Dalinar as they navigate political intrigue and ancient mysteries. In "Words of Radiance," Kaladin embraces his powers, Shallan uncovers dark secrets, and Dalinar strives to unite the kingdoms against the Voidbringers. Filled with epic battles and intricate plot twists, the series captivates with its rich world-building and complex characters, making it a modern epic fantasy masterpiece.
